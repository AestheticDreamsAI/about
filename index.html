The Real Story

No bullshit, just how it actually went down.

I'm 37, in a wheelchair since birth, and I've been building AI since 2003 - before "machine learning" was cool. While teachers told me what I'd never achieve, I was already coding my first pattern recognition system.

"Nobody believed in me - but I did."

The Real Journey (1997-2025)

1997 (age 10): Kidney transplant - my dad donated his kidney to save my life. Learned early that life doesn't give you easy choices, but it sometimes gives you second chances.

2003-2008: Started with basic pattern recognition while dealing with ongoing health issues. Built crude AI prototypes that barely worked, but got me hooked. Hip surgery during this time - excruciating pain with no proper medication taught me I could turn pain into power.

2009-2015: Went deep into C# and desktop automation. Built tons of personal productivity tools, UI experiments, small AI helpers - all for myself. Had a stroke that made me want to die, but talking about it openly with my parents saved me. This was my "learning in the shadows" phase.

2016-2020: Shifted focus to web technologies and voice interfaces. Built several voice-controlled systems for my own setup. Overcame severe chronic issues through intense fitness training that accomplished what 20 years of physiotherapy couldn't.

2021-2022: Started experimenting with modern ML frameworks and local AI. Built my first automated content systems as personal experiments. Finally began thinking "maybe other people would find this useful too."

2023-2024: Major personal transformation phase. Overcame porn addiction after 109 days of withdrawal, developed "Mindful Masculinity" concepts, transformed from "nice guy" to confident man with clear standards. Constantly working on personal development while building more sophisticated AI tools.

2025: Decided to stop building in the shadows. Released GoogolPlex 2025 - completely legal, using only official iOS features, with full documentation at gplex.dev. Launched Alessia Seelentanz as a live automated influencer. Started sharing my work openly after decades of private building, while continuing my personal growth journey.

"I spent decades building in private. Now I'm learning to build in public."

Why I Build Legal, Sustainable Tools

The original 2014 GoogolPlex hack was cool but doomed - it exploited Siri vulnerabilities that Apple quickly patched. My approach is different: build tools that use official features so they can't be killed. GoogolPlex 2025 uses iOS Shortcuts and Safari redirects - completely legal system functions that Apple can never remove without breaking their own OS.

I don't want to build something cool that disappears in 6 months when Apple releases an update. I want to build tools that people can rely on for years. Legal, sustainable, patch-proof.

What I'm Working On Now

GoogolPlex 2025 - a legal, patch-proof iOS voice assistant with growing user base, plugin ecosystem, and managed hosting coming soon. Automated influencers that create authentic content (still learning the viral growth game). Local-first AI that keeps your data on your devices where it belongs.

My Philosophy

Build tools that can't be killed. Use official features, respect user privacy, process locally. Your AI should run on YOUR devices with YOUR rules. No cloud dependencies, no data harvesting, no worrying about some company changing their API or shutting down your service.

I don't build AI to impress investors or follow trends. I build it to solve real problems for real people who want their tech to work reliably without selling their digital soul.
